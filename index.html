INSTRUCTIONS

Doors will be closed for arrival on January 14th, 2024. We implore you to start planning for this, clear up your schedules, and tidy up all the things needed before this day.

Once you have arrived, kindly note that you have to be in the camp all through the 3 days of the retreat. If you have to leave for any reason, you must inform and be permitted by one of the camp officials.

As much as possible, leave your phones, laptops, and all other electronic gadgets at home. If you must come with your phone, kindly note that the use of the phone is highly prohibited during the sessions.

Do not come with food items as your feeding will be taken care of.

There will be camping rules and we expect everyone to adhere to these rules.

Kindly come with your essentials; towel, spoon, plate, cup, toothbrush, toothpaste, soap, and so on

The retreat will be alongside fasting, from Friday morning till midnight on Sunday, light refreshments to break will be provided and a proper meal will also be provided later in the morning. Water, however, will be made available throughout. 

As much as is on our ability, let's do all we can to preserve the spiritual atmosphere of the retreat, this can mean no unnecessary gisting and jesting, and little or no interaction with the outside world, no social media.

FAQ

Will I be allowed to take liquid substances during the marathon fast?

Yes, Water will be freely available, however; carbonated drinks, fruit juice, smoothies, etc won't be permitted until after midnight on Sunday 

Can I leave the venue?

Yes, you can but with the express permission of one of the camp officials and you may or may not be allowed back into the camp.

Can I bring my younger siblings along?

No special provision will be made for children; they will go through the retreat the same way as adults. 

How much is the registration fee?

It is totally free. Feeding and accommodation will be taken care of however, registration is compulsory.

What if I have medical conditions that will not allow me to observe the fasting?

You can be exempted from fasting by the medical team during the retreat.
